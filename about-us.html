Who We Are

Gemini Logistics is Oahu's premier commercial logistics partner, specializing in the critical link between the port and your storefront. We don't just move freight; we provide the strategic infrastructure that keeps Hawaii's essential businesses running.

From temperature-controlled reefer transport for the island's top restaurants to heavy-duty dry freight for construction suppliers, our fleet and short-term warehousing solutions are built for Oahu's unique commercial landscape.

Our Story

Founded on the docks of Honolulu, Gemini Logistics was born from a simple observation: Oahu's commercial sector needed a more reliable, professional bridge between maritime arrivals and final delivery. We started with a single truck and a commitment to never miss a window.

Today, we have grown into a full-service logistics firm, yet our roots remain firmly planted in the port-to-store model. We understand the high stakes of Hawaii's supply chain because we've lived it every day since our inception.
